📍 Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Agra is a historic Indian city renowned for the magnificent Taj Mahal, a symbol of love and Mughal architecture. The city also features Agra Fort, bustling markets, rich heritage, traditional cuisine, and intricate marble craftsmanship, attracting millions of visitors from around the world each year.

🗺️ Top Places to Explore

The Taj Mahal

The iconic pure white marble monument of love, constructed beautifully under Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan.

Agra Fort

A massive, historic red sandstone walled fortress serving as the grand imperial seat of the Mughal Dynasty.

Fatehpur Sikri

The legendary ghost city outskirts featuring Buland Darwaza, the highest gateway architecture in the world.

🚌 Local Transport Options

  • Electric Rickshaws (E-Rickshaws): Approximately ₹30 - ₹80. Highly necessary since eco-pollution restrictions prohibit regular fuel cars around the immediate Taj layout.
  • Local App Cabs / Ola / Uber: Around ₹1,200 for full-day bookings. Seamless for transferring cleanly between distant monument complexes.

🏛️ Facts & Historical Specialities

  • The Taj Mahal completely alters its precise hue depending on the hours of the day—glowing soft pinkish in the early morning, milky white at noon, and golden under full moonlight.
  • Agra is universally famous for its "Petha", an authentic ultra-sweet translucent candy treat produced traditionally from ash gourd.
